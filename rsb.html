Food Safety

Food Safety Management System ISO 22000

With over two hundred diseases spread through the food chain, it's clear that
safe, sustainable food production is one of our greatest challenges.
Globalization of the food trade further complicates food safety and the new
edition of ISO 22000 on food safety management systems presents a timely
response.
